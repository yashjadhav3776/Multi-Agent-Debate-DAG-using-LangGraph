Topic: Should AI be regulated like medicine?
[Round 1] Scientist: AI must be regulated due to high-risk applications.
[Round 2] Philosopher: Regulation could stifle philosophical progress and autonomy.
[Round 3] Scientist: Medicine is regulated to protect humans; AI impacts can be similar.
[Round 4] Philosopher: Ethics evolve faster than laws; strict control may hinder adaptation.
[Round 5] Scientist: A framework like FDA can prevent harmful AI releases.
[Round 6] Philosopher: Creative AI breakthroughs often come from freedom, not control.
[Round 7] Scientist: Regulation ensures transparency and accountability.
[Round 8] Philosopher: History shows overregulation often delays societal evolution.
[Judge] Winner: Scientist
Reason: Presented more grounded, risk-based arguments aligned with public safety principles.
